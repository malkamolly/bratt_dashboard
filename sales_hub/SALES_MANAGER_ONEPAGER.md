# Sales Manager Quick Guide &mdash; Talking to Claude

## Logging in (one time)

1. Open **claude.ai/code** in your browser.
2. Sign in with **your Claude account** (your email).
3. Pick the project **`salesarborist_hub`**.
4. When Claude asks to connect GitHub, use the **bot login** the owner gave you. Never use those credentials on github.com directly &mdash; only inside Claude Code.

## What Claude does for you

Claude builds the Tuesday meeting page, files topics into the training library (with tags), updates the arborist roster, and updates the onboarding page. The website refreshes about 30 seconds after Claude saves.

## Each week, tell Claude four things

1. **Date** of the meeting (e.g. "Tuesday, May 19, 2026").
2. **Educational topic** &mdash; title plus your notes. Bullets or prose, both fine. Each sub-topic you list becomes a slide.
3. **Housekeeping** &mdash; timesheets, schedules, ride-alongs, etc.
4. **Operational updates** &mdash; CRM changes, promos, pricing, new services.

If a section doesn't apply this week, just say "no housekeeping this week."

## Example message you can copy

> Hey Claude, please build the meeting page for **Tuesday, May 19, 2026**.
>
> **Educational topic: Oak Wilt** &mdash; tag it Tree Disease and Diagnostics.
> - What it is &mdash; a fungal disease that kills oaks fast, especially red oaks.
> - How to spot it &mdash; leaves wilt from the top down, brown from edges in, drop while still partly green.
> - How it spreads &mdash; sap beetles in spring; root grafts between neighbors.
> - How to manage it &mdash; no pruning April-July; trench between infected and healthy trees; remove and burn infected wood.
> - Sales talking points &mdash; lead with prevention timing; offer trenching as a protective service.
>
> **Housekeeping:** Timesheets due Friday EOD. Ride-alongs wrap up next week.
>
> **Operational updates:** New oak wilt service line live in CRM, code OW-100. Spring promo ends June 15.

## Tips for good slides

- Short bullets beat long paragraphs &mdash; aim for 3 to 6 per sub-topic.
- Each sub-topic becomes its own slide, so group your notes that way.
- Reuse existing tag names. Ask Claude "what tags exist?" before inventing new ones.

## Privacy &mdash; the site is public

Do **not** ask Claude to publish full last names, personal phones, personal emails, home addresses, customer names, pricing, or internal numbers. If unsure, ask Claude "is this safe to put on the public site?"

## When something looks wrong

- **Page didn't update?** Wait 30 seconds and refresh.
- **Wording is off?** Tell Claude: "Change bullet 3 under 'How to manage it' to say&hellip;"
- **Want to undo something?** Tell Claude what to revert.

## One sentence to remember

> Tell Claude the date, the topic with your notes, the housekeeping bullets, and the operational updates. Claude does the rest.
