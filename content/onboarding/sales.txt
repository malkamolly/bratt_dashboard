@section-divider
number: 02
title: How You Get Paid
tagline: The pay structure for our sales team — and what we want you focused on first.


@three-rules
eyebrow: Pay Structure
title: Sales is commission — with a draw to start.
subtitle: Here's how the structure works.
rule: Weekly Draw | $1,250 per week ($60,000 annualized) goes into your account every week, no matter what.
rule: Monthly Commission | Commission is calculated and paid in the **first two weeks following a month**. Draw counts against it.
rule: First 90 Days | Focus on **learning the system and building your pipeline**. The numbers will follow.


@welcome
eyebrow: Pay Cadence
title: Weekly payroll. Monthly commission settles.
subtitle: One rhythm to settle into.
body: Payroll runs weekly through ADP — the **$1,250 draw** hits your account every week so you're not waiting on commission to live.
body: After the end of each month, we close out the month's commissions over the next two weeks and settle up. Your draw is deducted from what you've earned; the rest gets paid out.
