left: Applied via **soil drench or injection**

right-header: ArborPlex
right-icon: ●
right: **Liquid fertilizer with micronutrients**
right: Applied via **soil injection**
right: Best for **established trees needing nutrients**
right: Key ingredients: 14-4-5 NPK + micronutrients + humic acid
right: Function: boosts foliar and root growth, corrects nutrient deficiencies

@card-grid
eyebrow: When To Use NutriRoot
title: Pick NutriRoot when the tree is stressed or new.
subtitle: It's about establishment, recovery, and soil health.
card: Newly Planted | Recently transplanted or planted — establishment phase.
card: Drought Stressed | The biostimulant helps the tree pull more water from the soil.
card: Root Loss | After construction damage, root cutting, or major decline.
card: Poor / Compacted Soil | NutriRoot improves the soil environment around the roots, not just the tree.


@card-grid
eyebrow: When To Use ArborPlex
title: Pick ArborPlex when the tree is established but hungry.
subtitle: It's about feeding a tree that's otherwise healthy.
card: Established & Growing | Tree is past establishment, in active growth.
card: Nutrient Deficiency | Yellowing, weak canopy, slow growth not explained by water or roots.
card: Part Of A Program | Seasonal PHC where regular fertilization is part of the plan.
card: Urban Street Trees | Tough sites where soil nutrition is poor but roots are otherwise OK.


@section-divider
number: 02
title: Real-World Scenarios
tagline: How the choice plays out on actual client sites.


@card-grid
eyebrow: Pick The Right Product
title: Three common situations.
subtitle: When the answer is "one," "the other," or "both."
card: New Tree Install | **NutriRoot** for establishment. Add ArborPlex too if the soil is also poor.
card: Urban Street Tree, Poor Soil | **ArborPlex** for nutrient support. Add NutriRoot too if the tree shows root stress.
card: Construction-Damaged Tree | **NutriRoot first** for root repair, **then ArborPlex** as the tree starts to recover. Or both at once if the decline is fast.


@three-rules
eyebrow: Key Takeaways
title: How to prescribe like an arborist, not a salesman.
subtitle: Three rules that keep the conversation honest.
rule: Diagnose Before Prescribing | Walk the site, look at the tree, ask about history. **The condition determines the product** — don't lead with the product.
rule: NutriRoot = Soil + Water | When the issue is **roots, drought, or new establishment**, NutriRoot is the lever.
rule: ArborPlex = Feeding | When the issue is **a healthy tree that needs nutrients to thrive**, ArborPlex is the lever. Combine the two for phased recovery on stressed trees.


@closing
title: Diagnose the condition. Match the product.
subtitle: Sometimes both. Never neither.
